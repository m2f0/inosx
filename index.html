vii. FAQ

Before you ask — we've answered.

How does a project with INOSX start?

With a free diagnostic conversation. We map your context, objectives and data, and come back with a lean pilot proposal and a realistic timeline.

How long until results?

Typical pilots reach production in 4 to 8 weeks. The scope is defined to generate measurable value within that window.

Will my data be exposed?

No. We work with private architectures, local models when needed, data segregation and LGPD/GDPR compliance.

Do you integrate with our systems?

Yes. ERPs, CRMs, internal bases, APIs and legacy. Integration is part of scope, not a separate project.

Do you have a fixed tech stack?

No. We pick the right stack per project — Node, Python, Go, Rust on the back end; React, Svelte, Vue on the front; managed cloud or on-prem. Architecture follows your operational reality, not our preference. The same applies to AI: proprietary, open-source or local model, picked by the problem.

How is pricing?

Fixed scope for pilot and continuous-operation model for production. No hidden licenses, transparent costs.

Do you serve outside Brazil?

Yes. PT-BR, EN and ES, with global remote delivery.
